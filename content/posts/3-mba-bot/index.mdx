>###### I experimented with fine-tuned Llama 2 model using QLoRA. The early chatbot surfaced key lessons about cost, accuracy, and scalability.

This work was foundational to the progression of AI tutors at Harvard Business School. Which gave 930 MBA students access to an AI tutor in their core accounting course. Students used it over 20,000 times, mostly to break down concepts and practice problems. Faculty saw [stronger class discussions, better baseline knowledge, and improved midterm performance](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/siyarajpurohit_hbs-gave-its-mba-students-an-ai-tutor-activity-7350917084865880065-kHKc?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAAAmc1QABYgfhMh_fGLClxp8HUX53f6Sbcko).
